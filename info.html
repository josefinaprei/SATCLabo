About the series

"Sex and the City" is a popular American TV series that originally aired from 1998 to 2004. Set in New York City, it follows the lives of four close friends Carrie Bradshaw, Charlotte York, Miranda Hobbes, and Samantha Jone as they navigate the challenges of love, relationships, and careers in the big city. The show is centered around Carrie, a writer who pens a column about her dating experiences and observations on modern relationships.

Known for its bold take on romance, friendship, and sexuality, Sex and the City became iconic for its witty dialogue, fashionable style, and exploration of contemporary issues from a female perspective. The series' humor, emotional depth, and memorable characters turned it into a cultural phenomenon, leading to two movies and a reboot series, And Just Like That….
