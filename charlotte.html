Charlotte is the optimistic and traditional member of the group, believing in true love, marriage, and happily-ever-afters. A former art dealer, Charlotte is known for her classic style and adherence to social conventions. Though she starts out idealistic, her journey shows her resilience as she faces challenges with relationships, fertility, and her idea of what a perfect life should be. She has a big heart and a deep desire for family and stability.
